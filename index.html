what is pasabuy

PASABUY is a new and experimental platform that connects people that want to purchase products that are not available in their country, to people that traveling abroad.

As you know some products only available in some countries, but impossible to find in other`s. Or products that are very costly in one place will be very cheap in other.

PASABUY is a way for you to contact people that currently travelling the countries with the products you want make them an offer and get the things you want without the hassle of shipment or costumes.

How is it work

The best way to explain PASABUY is with a short example.

We have Joe, he lives in the Philippines and absolutely loves chocolate, unfortunately for Joe his favorite chocolate is only sold in Europe. So, Joe go`s to the PASABUY platform on Facebook and post that he would like to get his chocolate and he is willing to pay P1000 for his favorite chocolate.

And we have Dave, he is currently traveling Europe and after he saw the post of Joe, he went to the store and bout the chocolate for him. However, he only paid for the chocolate P600.


That means that by helping Joe Dave earned P400, while he was traveling.
